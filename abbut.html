How to Report GBV & TFGBV

1

Ensure safety first. Leave dangerous situations if possible and seek a trusted person.

➜
2

Document any evidence (messages, screenshots, photos, medical reports).

➜
3

Use listed hotlines or online platforms to report the case.

➜
4

Seek medical and psychological support at listed centers or clinics.

➜
5

Follow up with legal support or report to police stations with GBV desks.
